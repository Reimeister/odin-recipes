Bistek Filipino

A classic Filipino dish that's simple and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Thin slices of meat
  • Soy sauce
  • Calamansi juice (lemon juice as substitute)
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat for at least one hour before cooking.
  2. Pan fry marinated beef slices and half the onions (save marinade).
  3. Pull meat and onions out.
  4. Put remaining onions and garlic and stir fry.
  5. Put meat back in and cover to finish cooking.
  6. Top with the remaining onions.
