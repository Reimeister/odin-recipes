Try this delicious salmon recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 c. honey
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1/4 low-sodium soy sauce
  • red pepper flakes
  • 3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 4 6-oz. salmon fillets, patted dry with a paper towel
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves galric, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced into rounds

Instructions

  1. Whisk together honey, soy sauce, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Heat two tablespoons of oil in pan, then add salmon skin side up. Cook for 6 minutes until golden
  3. Add garlic to skillet and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add honey mixture and sliced lemon. Reduce sauce unless 1/3 is gone. Baste salmon with sauce
  5. Garnish with sliced lemon and serve
